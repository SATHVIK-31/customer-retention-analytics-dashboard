# Customer Retention Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project focuses on customer retention analytics using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to understand customer behavior, identify high-value customer segments, and develop actionable retention strategies.

---

## Project Workflow

1. Data Understanding
2. Feature Engineering
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
4. SQL-Based Business Analysis
5. Customer Segmentation
6. Retention Strategy Playbook
7. Interactive Power BI Dashboard

---

## Customer Segments

### VIP Loyalist
High-value customers with strong loyalty.

### Growth Customer
Customers with growth potential and increasing engagement.

### Bargain Hunter
Customers highly influenced by promotions and discounts.

### Low Engagement
Customers with lower purchase activity requiring re-engagement.

---

## Key Insights

- Bargain Hunters form the largest customer segment.
- VIP Loyalists generate the highest average revenue.
- Most customers show low promotion dependency.
- Customer value is distributed uniformly across value tiers.
